FAQ

How do I purchase tickets?

You can purchase tickets online at our website, or at the festival entrance.

What is the age limit for the festival?

You must be 18 or older to attend the festival.

What can I bring to the festival?

You can bring a small bag or backpack, but no outside food or drinks are allowed. We provide food and drink vendors on site.

Is there seating available?

Only VIP and Platinum ticket holders have access to seating areas.

Are pets allowed?

No, pets are not allowed at the festival.
